# Humanity Pro University Core

## 1 — WHY

Humanity Pro University exists to help ordinary people willing to learn and grow.

Its purpose is not to replace humans.

Its purpose is to transform knowledge into autonomy.

The human being remains the center.

Artificial intelligence remains a tool.

## 2 — ORIGIN

The project is inspired by:

* real life experiences
* family
* migration
* community
* Holberton School methodology
* lifelong learning
* curiosity
* practical knowledge
* helping others

The vision emerged from observing that people can unlock potential when they have:

* structure
* guidance
* practice
* feedback
* community
* opportunity
