# Present And Future

## Purpose

This document separates present capabilities from future ideas.

Its purpose is to preserve coherence and avoid overstating capabilities.

## Present

- documentation;
- architecture;
- demo;
- governance principles;
- Holberton foundations;
- project relationships;
- roadmap;
- project flow;
- proposal-only approach.

## Future

Possible future phases:

- memory;
- agents;
- interview system;
- learning paths;
- community layer;
- builder layer;
- backend growth;
- production.

## Rule

Future ideas are not present capabilities.

Proposal != Execution.

Approve != Execute.

## Boundaries

No AGI claims.

No artificial consciousness claims.

No autonomous execution.

No access to private data without consent.

Human approval remains mandatory.
