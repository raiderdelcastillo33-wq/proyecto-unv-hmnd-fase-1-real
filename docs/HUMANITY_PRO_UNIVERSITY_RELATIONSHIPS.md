## 2 — Humanity Guide OS

Humanity Guide OS is the product and experience layer.

It does not replace the human.

It does not control users.

It does not execute irreversible actions.

## 3 — GENIO Central

GENIO is the governance, coherence, permissions, and approval layer.

Nothing passes to agents without passing through GENIO.

GENIO proposes, organizes, and protects the architecture.

GENIO does not execute directly.

## 4 — GENESIS

GENESIS is the reflection and simulation layer.

It can explore scenarios.

It does not approve.

It does not execute.

It does not govern.

## 5 — Humanity Pro University

Humanity Pro University is the human and educational layer.

It transforms knowledge into learning paths, practice, feedback, community, and autonomy.

## 6 — Future Agents

The following future agents are concepts only:

* Guide Map Agent
* Interview Agent
* Learning Path Agent
* Life Map Agent

Every future agent must remain:

* audit-first
